Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 1, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to StarCypher.xyz! We value your trust and take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your personal information when using our Telegram bot.

By using the StarCypher bot, you agree to the terms described in this policy. If you do not agree with any provisions, please do not use our service.

2. Data We Collect

To provide quality astrological forecasts, we collect the following information:

2.1 Required Data

  • Telegram ID - unique identifier of your Telegram account
  • Username - your name and username in Telegram
  • Messages - questions and information you send to the bot
  • Date and time of interaction - timestamps of your requests

2.2 Optional Data (for personalization)

  • Birth date - for creating birth charts and accurate predictions
  • Birth time and place - for in-depth astrological analysis (VIP)
  • Personal questions - information about love, career that you voluntarily provide
Important: We DO NOT collect your phone number, contacts, real-time geolocation, or other data without your explicit consent.

3. How We Use Your Data

The collected information is used exclusively for the following purposes:

  • Astrological analysis - creating personalized forecasts and predictions using Kimi AI
  • Service improvement - analyzing popular queries to improve response quality
  • Technical support - helping solve issues and answering your questions
  • Subscription management - processing payments and managing VIP status
  • Security - preventing fraud and abuse

We DO NOT sell your data to third parties. We DO NOT use your data for advertising without your consent.

4. AI Data Processing

StarCypher uses Kimi AI technology to analyze astrological aspects and generate personalized forecasts. Your messages may be processed by the AI system to:

  • Understand the context of your questions
  • Create relevant astrological interpretations
  • Form personalized recommendations

Data transmitted to the AI system is protected and used only to generate responses for you. It is not used for model training without your consent.

5. Data Storage and Security

5.1 Where Data is Stored

Your data is stored on secure servers using modern encryption technologies. We apply industry-standard security measures to protect information from unauthorized access.

5.2 Retention Period

  • Message history: 90 days for Free users, 1 year for VIP
  • Personal data: as long as you actively use the bot
  • After account deletion: data is deleted within 30 days

5.3 Security Measures

  • Data encryption in transit (SSL/TLS)
  • Data encryption at rest
  • Limited data access only for authorized personnel
  • Regular security audits and updates

6. Your Rights

In accordance with data protection laws, you have the following rights:

  • Right to access: request a copy of all data we store about you
  • Right to rectification: correct inaccurate information
  • Right to erasure: delete all your data from the system

  • Right to object: object to certain types of processing
  • Right to data portability: receive data in a structured format
How to exercise your rights:
Send the command /delete_my_data in the bot or contact us via email below.
